Privacy Policy

Med Tracker Mobile Application

Developed by: Gleb Andreev (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "our").

1. Core Principles and Operating Mode

The Med Tracker application (the "Service") is designed with absolute user privacy as a priority. This Privacy Policy informs you about our data processing practices.

Key Operating Principles of Med Tracker:

  • No Backend Server Component: The Service operates completely standalone. We do not utilize remote servers, cloud storage, or third-party APIs to store user data.
  • No Authorization: We do not require and do not offer users the option to register accounts or go through any authorization process.
  • No Data Transmission: User data is never collected, processed, or transmitted by us or third parties outside of the user's device.

2. Information Collection and Storage

2.1. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect and do not store the following types of data:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Financial information.
  • Personal Health Information (PHI) or any information regarding your medical status.
2.2. Locally Stored Data (User Data)

All inventory information you enter into the Med Tracker application is stored exclusively on your mobile device in a local database. We do not have access to this data.

Locally stored user data includes:

  • Medication names and brief descriptions (user-entered).
  • Medication type (selected by the user from a predefined list).
  • Expiration dates.
  • Any additional notes entered by the user.
errorImportant

Since all data is stored locally, we cannot recover your data if the application is uninstalled, device settings are reset, or the device is replaced.

2.3. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or other services that would collect data. The application uses only the minimum operating system services required for local functionality.

3. Permissions and Notifications

To enable the core feature of the Service — expiration date reminders — Med Tracker requests permission from the operating system to send local notifications.

4. Children's Privacy

As Med Tracker does not collect any personal information and does not set age restrictions, it complies with privacy requirements for applications that do not collect children's personal data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated Policy will be published on this page. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically.
